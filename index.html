How to Play

✏️

Draw Shapes

Use your mouse or finger to draw shapes on the canvas. Freehand, rectangles, circles, and planks are available.

🎯

Reach the Goal

Guide the ball into the green target. Use physics creatively — ramps, weights, bridges all work!

⭐

Earn Stars

Complete levels faster and with fewer shapes to earn 3 stars. Stars unlock bonus coins.

🔴

Red Zones

Red areas are forbidden — you cannot draw inside them. Plan your shapes around the boundaries.

💡

Use Clues

Stuck? Spend coins to reveal a clue. Earn coins by completing levels quickly and with few shapes.
